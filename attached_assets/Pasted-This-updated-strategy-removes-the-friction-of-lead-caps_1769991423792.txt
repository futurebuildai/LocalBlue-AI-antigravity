The "Graduated Trial" Logic
This two-step trial moves them through the "Aha! Moment" without asking for a card until they are ready to commit their brand to the platform.
Phase 1: The "Test Drive" (30 Days)
 * Domain: yourbusiness.localblue.co
 * Requirement: No Credit Card.
 * Goal: Let them see the AI-generated site and play with the admin panel.
 * Psychology: This removes all risk. It lets them "feel" the white-label admin without spending a dime.
Phase 2: The "Professional Launch" (14 Days)
 * Domain: www.yourbusiness.com (Custom Domain)
 * Requirement: Credit Card Required to publish.
 * Logic: Connecting a domain requires technical resources (SSL provisioning, DNS management). By requiring a card here, you filter for "serious" contractors who are ready to go live.
 * Safety Net: They still get 14 days of the custom domain for free before the first charge hits.
